About me

I got my Bachelor's degree at Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, department of Applied Mathematics and Control. Having received extensive training in fundamental fields of mathematics and physics, I decided to gain understanding of processes governing institutions from levels of individuals to international organizations’ levels. I entered program Master of Arts in Economics at New Economic School, leading Russian university in the field of economics. After graduation I started to apply my knowledge at Alfa-Bank and then at Data Nerds. My focus there was on applying cutting edge research in statistics and machine learning for risk modelling, pricing, demand analysis and various types of predictions for large financial institutions and retail companies, as well as carrying some internal research. You can find brief description of my previous work projects in the Work Projects section.

Recently I've started exploring the field of quantum computing which together with statistical learning represent my scientific interests. See description of my early independent research projects in Research & Software section. In pursuit of these interests, I entered Russian Quantum Center as a research software engineer.
